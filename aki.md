## AKIKI Trial (2016)
**Journal:** NEJM  
**Design:** Multicenter RCT, n=620  
**Population:** Severe AKI in ICU  
**Intervention:** Early RRT  
**Comparator:** Delayed RRT  
**Primary Outcome:** 60-day mortality  
**Key Results:** No difference in mortality  
**Reference:** Gaudry S et al., NEJM 2016. PubMed

## ELAIN Trial (2016)
**Journal:** JAMA  
**Design:** Single-center RCT, n=231  
**Population:** AKI stage 2  
**Intervention:** Early RRT  
**Comparator:** Delayed RRT  
**Primary Outcome:** 90-day mortality  
**Key Results:** Reduced mortality with early RRT  
**Reference:** Zarbock A et al., JAMA 2016. PubMed

## STARRT-AKI (2020)
**Journal:** NEJM  
**Design:** Multicenter RCT, n=3019  
**Population:** Critically ill with severe AKI  
**Intervention:** Accelerated RRT  
**Comparator:** Standard RRT  
**Primary Outcome:** 90-day mortality  
**Key Results:** No difference in mortality  
**Reference:** STARRT-AKI Investigators, NEJM 2020. PubMed
