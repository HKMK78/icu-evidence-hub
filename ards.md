# Acute Respiratory Distress Syndrome – Landmark Trials

## ARDSNet Low Tidal Volume (2000)
**Journal:** NEJM  
**Design:** Multicenter RCT, n=861  
**Population:** ARDS  
**Intervention:** 6 mL/kg predicted body weight tidal volume  
**Comparator:** 12 mL/kg tidal volume  
**Primary Outcome:** Mortality  
**Key Results:** Mortality 31% vs 39% (p<0.007)  
**Reference:** ARDSNet, NEJM 2000. PubMed

## PROSEVA Trial (2013)
**Journal:** NEJM  
**Design:** Multicenter RCT, n=474  
**Population:** Severe ARDS  
**Intervention:** Prone positioning >16h/day  
**Comparator:** Supine position  
**Primary Outcome:** 28-day mortality  
**Key Results:** 16% vs 32.8% mortality  
**Reference:** Guérin C et al., NEJM 2013. PubMed

## Fluid Management in ARDS (FACTT 2006)
**Journal:** NEJM  
**Design:** Multicenter RCT, n=1000  
**Population:** ARDS  
**Intervention:** Conservative fluid strategy  
**Comparator:** Liberal fluid strategy  
**Primary Outcome:** Ventilator-free days  
**Key Results:** More ventilator-free days, no mortality increase  
**Reference:** FACTT Study, NEJM 2006. PubMed
