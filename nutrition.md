## EDEN Trial (2012)
**Journal:** NEJM  
**Design:** Multicenter RCT, n=1000  
**Population:** Acute lung injury  
**Intervention:** Trophic enteral feeding  
**Comparator:** Full enteral feeding  
**Primary Outcome:** Ventilator-free days  
**Key Results:** No difference in outcomes  
**Reference:** EDEN Investigators, NEJM 2012. PubMed

## EPaNIC Trial (2011)
**Journal:** NEJM  
**Design:** Multicenter RCT, n=4640  
**Population:** Critically ill adults  
**Intervention:** Early parenteral nutrition  
**Comparator:** Late parenteral nutrition  
**Primary Outcome:** ICU length of stay  
**Key Results:** Late PN associated with faster recovery  
**Reference:** Casaer MP et al., NEJM 2011. PubMed

## PermiT Trial (2015)
**Journal:** NEJM  
**Design:** Multicenter RCT, n=894  
**Population:** ICU patients  
**Intervention:** Permissive underfeeding  
**Comparator:** Standard feeding  
**Primary Outcome:** 90-day mortality  
**Key Results:** No mortality difference  
**Reference:** Arabi YM et al., NEJM 2015. PubMed
