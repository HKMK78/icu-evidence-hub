## NICE-SUGAR Trial (2009)
**Journal:** NEJM  
**Design:** Multicenter RCT, n=6104  
**Population:** Critically ill patients  
**Intervention:** Intensive glucose control  
**Comparator:** Conventional glucose control  
**Primary Outcome:** 90-day mortality  
**Key Results:** Higher mortality with intensive control  
**Reference:** NICE-SUGAR Study, NEJM 2009. PubMed

## CHECKLIST-ICU Trial (2018)
**Journal:** NEJM  
**Design:** Cluster-randomized trial  
**Population:** ICU patients  
**Intervention:** Daily checklist with goal setting  
**Comparator:** Standard care  
**Primary Outcome:** In-hospital mortality  
**Key Results:** No significant difference  
**Reference:** CHECKLIST-ICU Investigators, NEJM 2018. PubMed

## ICU Liberation (ABCDE Bundle Studies)
**Journal:** Various  
**Design:** Observational and interventional studies  
**Outcome:** Reduced delirium, shorter ventilation  
**Reference:** Society of Critical Care Medicine publications
