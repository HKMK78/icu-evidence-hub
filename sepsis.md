# Sepsis & Septic Shock – Landmark Trials

## Rivers et al. – Early Goal-Directed Therapy (2001)
**Journal:** New England Journal of Medicine

**Design**
- Randomized, single-center
- n = 263

**Population**
- Severe sepsis and septic shock

**Intervention**
- Early goal-directed therapy protocol

**Comparator**
- Standard care

**Primary Outcome**
- In-hospital mortality

**Key Results**
- Reduced mortality in intervention group

**Key Limitations**
- Single-center
- Outdated standard care

**Reference**
- Rivers E et al. NEJM 2001. PubMed
