# Systematic Reviews & Meta-analyses in Critical Care

## Fluid Strategies in Sepsis
**Question:** Conservative vs liberal fluid strategies  
**Studies Included:** Multiple RCTs  
**Key Findings:** Conservative strategies reduce ventilator days without increasing mortality  
**Reference:** Cochrane Review, PubMed

## Timing of Renal Replacement Therapy
**Question:** Early vs delayed initiation of RRT  
**Key Findings:** No consistent mortality benefit with early initiation  
**Reference:** STARRT-AKI meta-analyses, PubMed

## Sedation Strategies
**Question:** Light vs deep sedation  
**Key Findings:** Light sedation associated with better neurological outcomes  
**Reference:** Systematic reviews in Critical Care Medicine
